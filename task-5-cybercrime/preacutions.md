# 🔐 Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)

## ✅ Prevention Tips

- ✔️ Use strong, unique passwords for each account (include numbers, symbols, and uppercase letters)  
- ✔️ Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on email, banking, and social media accounts  
- ✔️ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into sensitive accounts (banking, email, etc.)  
- ✔️ Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software  
- ✔️ Be cautious of phishing emails, fake job offers, and suspicious links (especially on WhatsApp & Instagram)  
- ✔️ Limit the amount of personal information shared on social media profiles  
- ✔️ Always download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store  

### 💰 Financial / UPI Safety (Important)

- ✔️ Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone — banks never ask for this  
- ✔️ Double-check UPI IDs and payment details before sending money  
- ✔️ Avoid scanning unknown QR codes or accepting random “collect requests”  
- ✔️ Use official banking apps and verify merchant authenticity before transactions  

---

## 🚫 What to Avoid

- ❌ Clicking on unknown links or attachments  
- ❌ Sharing passwords or OTPs with friends or strangers  
- ❌ Saving passwords on shared/public devices  
- ❌ Falling for “too good to be true” offers or giveaways  

---

## 🚨 Report Cyber Crime

If you encounter any online fraud, harassment, or cybercrime:

- 🌐 **National Cyber Crime Portal:** https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  
- 📞 **Helpline Number:** 1930  

👉 Report immediately to increase chances of recovery in financial fraud cases.
