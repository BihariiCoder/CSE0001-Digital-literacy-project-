## 📌 Project Overview
This project is part of the **CSE0001 Digital Literacy course**.  
It focuses on spreading awareness about **safe and responsible digital practices**, especially for students in India.

The project covers key topics such as:
- Responsible social media usage  
- Online safety and cyber hygiene  
- Cybercrime awareness (UPI fraud, phishing, etc.)  
- Preventive measures and reporting mechanisms  

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- To educate students about digital safety  
- To promote responsible use of social media  
- To raise awareness about common cyber threats  
- To provide actionable safety guidelines  

---

## 🧠 Topics Covered

### 📱 Responsible Social Media Use
- Do’s and Don’ts for students  
- Managing digital footprint  

### 🔐 Stay Safe Online
- Cyber safety checklist  
- UPI and financial fraud prevention tips  

### ⚠️ Cybercrime Case Study
- Realistic example of UPI fraud  
- Step-by-step explanation of how scams happen
