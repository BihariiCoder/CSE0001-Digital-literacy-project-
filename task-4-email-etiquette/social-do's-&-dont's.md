# 📱 Responsible Social Media Use (College Students)

## ✅ Do's
- ✔️ Think before you post — consider long-term impact  
- ✔️ Maintain privacy settings on your accounts  
- ✔️ Share positive, respectful, and meaningful content  
- ✔️ Verify information before sharing (avoid fake news)  
- ✔️ Use social media for learning, networking, and growth  

## ❌ Don'ts
- ❌ Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly  
- ❌ Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online harassment  
- ❌ Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content  
- ❌ Don’t believe or spread unverified information  
- ❌ Don’t let social media affect your studies or mental health
